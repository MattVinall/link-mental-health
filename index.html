About Us

LinkMentalHealth was co-founded by two brothers who are motivated by the challenges of finding help. Our platform connects people to in-peson therapy services regardless of their budget. We have a vision to make mental health support accessible to everyone who needs or wants it. We are starting with 4.75 million Canadians who are struggling with a mental health condition but not getting the help they need
